Welcome to Serena SDA Church

Serena SDA Church is a vibrant community of faith located in. We are dedicated to worship, service, and sharing the love of Christ with everyone. Join us every Sabbath for uplifting sermons, engaging Bible studies, and fellowship.

Experience the joy of worship every Sabbath at Serena SDA Church. Our services include inspiring sermons, soulful music, and opportunities to connect with our loving church family.
